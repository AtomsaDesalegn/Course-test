How to type fast?

There is no shortcut, you need to practice hard to increase your typing speed. This online typing test platform provides you all the necessary exercises and training materials. But you need to practice regularly and follow the rules of typing. Here are some primary facts that can boost your typing speed.

Steps to follow to type fast

  1. Use the touch typing method. Although there are several other typing methods available, touch typing is the fastest and best. Almost every typist uses this way of typing. So, if you want to make your typing fast, learn touch typing first.
  2. The keyboard is the primary tool to type. And if you use a fancy keyboard that is not supportive of your fast finger movement, your typing speed will never improve. We recommend a mechanical or optical keyboard to practice
  3. Having the right sitting position is very serious when you practice for a long time. If you sit comfortably, you can easily type for a long time.

These three steps can help you to make your typing fast and more accurate. To know all the facts that can affect your typing speed, read this article: How to increase typing speed.

How to learn touch typing quickly?

The touch typing is a typing method where the typist feels the keys and type without looking at the keyboard. It’s a complicated process to memorize the keyboard layout and type without looking at the keys. In this case, our systematic learning exercises can help you a lot to learn & acquire this method. If you are a beginner, you may play some typing games to set your fingers on the appropriate keys.
